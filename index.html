We're Hiring

Barista

    Job Duties:
  • Prepare all menu items to the best of employees ability, always striving to improve and serve an incredible product
  • Serve customers with hospitality, kindness and approachability, always explaining the menu as much or as little as the customer needs
  • Keep a clean and tidy workspace, always cleaning in free time, even inside and behind items, following the cleaning checklists closely
  • Be a team player and strive to work well with other employees
  • Be prompt and on time for shifts
  • Adhere to the rules and standards HipJoe has set for all employees
      Education and/or Work Experience Requirements:
  • Prior coffee experience a plus
  • Exceptional interpersonal skills and decision making ability
  • Willingness and availability to work a flexible schedule that may include nights and weekends
      • Compensation
  • $12 per hour
  • Baristas earn an additional $3-4 per hour in tips on top of base wage
  • Full time employees are eligible for health, dental, and vision coverage. Employer pays 50% of premium.
      • Hours
  • Both full-time and part-time positions are available.
